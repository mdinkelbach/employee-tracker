When start, get options to:
	View all departments
	View all roles
	view all employees
	Add a department
	Add a role
	Add an employee
	Update employee role
When choose to view departments:
	Table showing department names and ids
When choose to view roles:
	Table showing job title, role id, department of role, salary 	of role
When choose to view employees:
	Table showing employee id, first name, last name, title, 	department, salary, manager
When choose to add department
	Enter department name
When choose to add role
	Enter role name, role salary, and department role belongs to
When choose to add employee
	Enter first name, last name, role, and manager
When choose to update employee
	Choose employee, new role
